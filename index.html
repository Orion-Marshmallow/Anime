FAQ

GOT QUESTIONS?
WE'VE GOT ANSWERS.

Everything you need to know before you trust us with your payments.

What is Trustana and how does it work? ▾
Trustana is a freelance escrow platform for Africa. When a client hires a freelancer, the client locks payment — settled in USDC, funded via Crypto or Fiat in any currency — into a secure escrow before work begins. The freelancer delivers the work and submits a delivery link. Our SmartVerify™ AI checks the delivery against the original requirements and scores it. If everything checks out, the client releases the funds — or they auto-release after 24 hours if the client takes no action.
Is my money safe? What if something goes wrong? ▾
Yes. Funds are locked in escrow and cannot be accessed by anyone until the escrow is resolved. If there's a dispute, funds freeze for 48 hours and both sides submit their case. Our AI-assisted arbitration process reviews both sides fairly and issues a binding recommendation. Neither party can simply take the money and run.
What currencies and payment methods are supported? ▾
Every escrow on Trustana settles in USDC, a dollar-pegged stablecoin — so funds never lose value while sitting in escrow. You can fund an escrow two ways: crypto-native, sending USDC directly via Phantom wallet, or Fiat via Paystack — Naira, USD, GBP, and more, by card, bank transfer, or USSD — which we automatically convert to USDC. More payment methods are on our roadmap.
What is SmartVerify™ and can it be fooled? ▾
SmartVerify™ is powered by Claude AI (Anthropic). When a freelancer submits work, Claude reads the original job description and requirements, then checks each one against the delivery link and notes — scoring pass, partial, or fail per requirement. It's not foolproof (no AI is), but it provides an objective, bias-free first pass that removes the human tendency to be swayed by excuses or pressure. The final decision always involves human context.
What happens if the client disappears after I deliver? ▾
This is exactly why Trustana exists. Once you submit your delivery, an auto-release timer starts (24 hours). If the client takes no action — doesn't approve, doesn't dispute — the funds are automatically released to you. No chasing. No ghosting. You get paid regardless.
How much does Trustana cost? ▾
Creating an account is completely free. Trustana charges a 2.5% platform fee only on successfully released escrows — nothing on cancellations or disputes. Growth tier (1.8%) applies once you're doing 3+ jobs a month. See full pricing →
Is Trustana available outside Nigeria? ▾
Trustana is Nigeria-first but not Nigeria-only. The Crypto rail works globally — any client anywhere in the world can lock Crypto. The Fiat rail supports Naira and a growing list of other currencies via Paystack, with more African markets — Ghana, Kenya, South Africa — being added continuously. If you're in the diaspora, Crypto payments work for you right now.
Do I need a crypto wallet to use Trustana? ▾
No. If you're using Fiat payments, you don't need a crypto wallet at all — just a Nigerian bank account or card via Paystack. A Solana wallet (like Phantom) is only required for Crypto payments. You can also add wallet addresses manually if you don't have Phantom installed.

THE BACKSTORY

In early 2026, a member of our team delivered a full React project for a UK-based client — three weeks of work. The client disappeared. No payment. No response. No recourse.

We looked for an escrow solution. Escrow.com was expensive and slow. PayPal had no Nigeria support. Crypto escrows were too technical for most clients. Nothing worked for the Nigerian freelance context.

So we built Trustana in a weekend. Then kept building. SmartVerify™ — our AI delivery verification engine — was the breakthrough. Instead of trusting a human arbiter, the AI checks the delivery against the original requirements and issues a verdict. No bias. No delay. No politics.

We're building this for the Chidera Okafors of Nigeria — talented developers, designers, and creatives who deserve to get paid every single time.

WHAT IS TRUSTANA?

Trustana is Nigeria's first AI-powered freelance escrow protocol. A client locks payment before any work begins, the freelancer delivers, and SmartVerify™ checks the submission against the original brief before funds are released — automatically, in seconds, with no human gatekeeper on either side.

Every escrow settles in USDC — a dollar-pegged stablecoin that never loses value while your funds sit in escrow. Fund it however suits you: crypto-native via Phantom wallet, or Fiat via Paystack — Naira, USD, GBP, and more — automatically converted to USDC. Milestone-based releases, built-in dispute resolution, and WhatsApp delivery alerts mean both sides always know exactly where their money and their work stand.

Every escrow is powered by Solana under the hood for instant, low-fee, on-chain settlement — so a client in London can lock funds for a developer in Lagos in under 30 seconds, with zero wire fees and zero currency friction.
